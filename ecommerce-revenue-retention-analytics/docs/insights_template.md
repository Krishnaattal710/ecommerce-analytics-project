# Insights Template (Use in README + Interview)

1. Revenue Trend:
- Monthly revenue increased/decreased by `X%` in `Month` vs previous month.

2. Category Insight:
- `Category A` contributed `Y%` of delivered-order revenue.

3. Customer Behavior:
- Repeat purchase rate is `R%`, indicating `high/moderate/low` retention.

4. Operations Insight:
- `State S` has the worst on-time delivery (`T%`) and average delay (`D days`).

5. Risk Signal:
- Failed order rate peaked at `F%` in `Month`, likely linked to `status/capacity/seasonality`.

## Recommendation examples
- Improve fulfillment for lagging states via partner-level SLA targets.
- Prioritize high-margin top categories in campaigns.
- Launch retention campaigns for first-time buyers in month 1-2.
